# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two audiences: (1) Wickie's followers and home cooks landing from Instagram/TikTok who want her recipes and cookbook picks; (2) brands and partners evaluating her for collaborations. Secondary user: Wickie herself, a zero-technical editor who manages every word and photo through TinaCMS at /admin.

## Product Purpose

Personal brand + creator site for Wickie Fort / Wickie's Kitchen (wickiefort.com): home for her recipes, story, work-with-me offerings, ShopMy cookbook shelf, and social links. Success = followers find recipes easily, brands can reach her in one click, and Wickie can edit everything herself without breaking anything.

## Brand Personality

Warm, simple, real. "Less is more, and simple is better" is her stated philosophy — the design mirrors it: handwritten accents, serif display type, warm palette, food photography doing the heavy lifting.

## Anti-references

- Cluttered food-blog templates (ad-stuffed, popup-heavy, endless scroll before the recipe)
- Generic SaaS/landing-page aesthetics — this is a person, not a product
- Anything Wickie can't edit herself through /admin

## Design Principles

1. **The recipe is the product** — get readers to steps and ingredients fast; chrome stays minimal.
2. **Wickie-proof editing** — every section survives whatever she pastes or reorders in TinaCMS; layout never depends on content being well-formed.
3. **Warmth through type and photo, not decoration** — serif + handwritten accents on a warm ground; her photos carry the personality.
4. **One page, orderable** — the homepage is sections Wickie can hide/reorder; each section must stand alone at any position and any width.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target. Reveal animations honor prefers-reduced-motion (visible-by-default, JS opts in). Semantic landmarks, alt text on media, keyboard-reachable controls.
